How to Play

  • Memorize the cards when they are revealed for 5 seconds at the start.
  • You have 15 moves to match all the cats.
  • Click two cards to flip them over.
  • If they match, they stay open; if not, they flip back.
  • Match all pairs before the timer runs out to win.
